    Degemer mat !

    I am a post doctoral researcher at ENS Lyon within the LIP laboratory in the DANTE team. I am currently working on compressive learning and sketching methods.

    I was previously working on Optimal Transport methods for machine learning and I obtained my PhD in November 2020. I was supervised by Nicolas Courty, Laetitia Chapel an Romain Tavenard within the OBELIX team of IRISA in Vannes. You can find the manuscript of my thesis here: A contribution to Optimal Transport on incomparable spaces.

    Misc.

    I help organizing the Machine Learning and Signal Processing seminar (MLSP) in ENS Lyon. Feel free to contact me if you want to present your work !
